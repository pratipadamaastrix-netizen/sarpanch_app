About us

The Sarpanch / Municipality Directory is a web portal that brings together location-wise information about elected representatives—Sarpanchs and chairpersons—linked to villages and municipalities across the hierarchy of districts, constituencies, and mandals.

What you can do

  • Public visitors can browse high-level directory statistics and featured updates on the home page.
  • Registered users can log in to search representatives by location and access extended features subject to subscription.
  • Sarpanchs can use the dedicated OTP-based login to access and maintain their profile where enabled.

Our goal

To make representative information easier to discover and to support transparent access to local governance contacts for citizens, administrators, and partner organisations.

Content and counts on this site are maintained through the administrative console and update automatically as data changes.
